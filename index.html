Our Story

Nearly 50 Years of Doing Right by Dayton

Big Muffler Shop opened its doors on N Keowee Street back in 1976 and quickly became the place Dayton drivers trust for exhaust work that lasts. What started as a muffler shop grew into a full-service repair garage handling brakes, suspension, front-end work and most everyday repairs.

In 2016, longtime hands Richard Deitz and Chris Kelty took over the business — keeping the same straight-shooting approach that built the shop's reputation in the first place. Customers come back year after year because the work is solid and the pricing is honest.

  • Locally owned and operated for nearly five decades
  • Foreign and domestic — all makes and models
